# Student Hub

Student Hub is a comprehensive web platform designed to cater to the diverse needs of students. It offers information and resources on various aspects of student life, including accommodation, dining, commuting, consultancies, and tourist attractions.

## Table of Contents

- [Features](#features)
- [Installation](#installation)
- [Usage](#usage)
- [Contributing](#contributing)
- [License](#license)

## Features

- **Hostels**: Find information about student hostels, including locations and amenities.
- **Eatery**: Discover dining options suitable for students.
- **Commute**: Learn about transportation options and commuting tips.
- **Consultancies**: Access a list of consultancies offering services to students.
- **Tourist Attractions**: Explore local tourist spots and recreational activities.
